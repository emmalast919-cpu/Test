Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Aimo is packed with features that make managing music in your server effortless and enjoyable.

High Quality Audio

Stream music in crystal-clear quality from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and many other sources.

Playlist Management

Create, save, share, and load playlists. Import from Spotify and manage tracks with ease.

Favourites

Save your favorite songs and play them instantly. Build your personal music collection.

Audio Filters

Apply equalizer presets, bass boost, reverb, echo, speed control, and stereo effects to your music.

24/7 Mode

Keep Aimo in your voice channel around the clock. Continuous music without interruption.

AI Assistant

Ask Aimo anything using the built-in AI. Get song recommendations, trivia, and more.

Spotify Integration

Login with your Spotify account, play your playlists, and view your profile directly in Discord.

Mini Games

Take a break with Snake, Minesweeper, Trivia, and Rock Paper Scissors — all inside Discord.

Lyrics Support

Display real-time lyrics for the currently playing song so everyone can sing along.
