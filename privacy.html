Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This bot is a Discord music bot designed to provide music playback and related features. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

To function properly, the bot may collect:

User Data

  • User IDs
  • Usernames and tags

Used for features like playlists, favorites, and command responses.

Server Data

  • Server (Guild) IDs
  • Server-specific settings (e.g., prefix, autoplay, configurations)

Message Content

The bot only reads messages that:

  • Are commands sent to the bot
  • Mention the bot

The bot does not store or monitor normal conversations.

Music Data

  • Song titles, URLs, and queue information
  • Playback status (current song, duration, etc.)

This data is temporary and only used for music functionality.

How We Use Data

Data is used only to:

  • Provide music playback and queue management
  • Save user and server settings
  • Maintain and improve performance
  • Prevent misuse or abuse

Data Storage

  • Data is stored securely in a private database
  • Temporary data (like music queues) is automatically deleted
  • Data is removed when the bot leaves a server

Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept the changes.

Agreement

By using this bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions, join our support server and our team will be happy to help.
