All Aimo Commands

Over 60 commands across 9 categories. Use the filter below to find what you need.

Music Playback
15 commands
/playPlay a song or playlist from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and more.
/skipSkip the current song and play the next one in the queue.
/pausePause the currently playing song.
/resumeResume a paused song.
/stopStop playback and clear the queue.
/nowplayingShow detailed info about the currently playing song.
/lyricsDisplay real-time lyrics for the current or searched song.
/seekSeek to a specific timestamp in the current song.
/rewindRewind the current song by a specified number of seconds.
/fastforwardFast forward the current song by a specified number of seconds.
/replayRestart the current song from the beginning.
/disconnectDisconnect Aimo from the voice channel.
/247Toggle 24/7 mode — keep Aimo in your voice channel around the clock.
/autoplayToggle autoplay to keep music going after the queue ends.
/loopSet loop mode: off, current song, or entire queue.
